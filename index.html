About Us, powering the renewable evolution

The GreenCape Initiative embodies the strategic intent of the Provincial Government of the Western Cape to ensure that the Western Cape captures a significant share of the jobs created in the renewable and green economy, and is at the forefront of renewable energy and green technology.
